Frequently Asked Questions

Are British Shorthairs cuddly?

Yes! While they aren't always "velcro cats" that demand to be carried constantly, they are incredibly affectionate and love to sit beside you (or on your feet) while you relax.

Do they get along with kids and dogs?

Absolutely. Their easygoing, calm, and patient temperament makes them fantastic companions for families with children and cat-friendly dogs.
